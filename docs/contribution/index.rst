Contribution
------------
Everyone is welcome, each small contribution, no matter if it is a fix of a typo in the
documentation, bug report, an idea, or a question, is valuable. As a member of our community, you
should adhere to the principles presented in the :doc:`code-of-conduct`.

If you'd like to contribute, please read the :doc:`contributing`. It will help you to understand the
way geotech-pandas development works and us to review your contribution.
